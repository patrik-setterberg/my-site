Notes on post formatting

Markdown syntax micro cheat-sheet:

  • Emphasized text - *single asterisks* or _single underlines_.
  • Bold text - **double asterisks** or __double underlines__.
  • Links by [link text](url).
  • code.py - `backtick quotes`.
  • Inline image - ![Alt text](/path/to/img.jpg "Optional title").
  • Blockquote - > quote.
  • Escape characters (e.g. _, *, `) with backslash (\*, \_, \`).
